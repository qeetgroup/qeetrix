Add the full Tailwind v4 color palette (21 ramps × 50–950) as `--qx-color-<name>-<step>` primitive tokens — red, orange, amber, yellow, lime, green, emerald, teal, cyan, sky, blue, indigo, violet, purple, fuchsia, pink, rose, slate, gray, zinc, stone. Exposed in `@qeetrix/ui/tokens.css` (raw) and `@qeetrix/ui/tokens.json`, and documented in Foundations → Colors as a palette gallery. Purely additive: existing ramps (`neutral`, the semantic `success`/`warning`/`info`/`danger` status ramps, brand placeholders) and all semantic mappings are unchanged.
